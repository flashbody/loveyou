1. Introduction

LoveYou ("we", "our", or "the App") is a personal safety guardian application designed for people living alone. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

Location Data — We collect your device's location in the background to provide safety monitoring. Location data is cached locally on your device and included in emergency alert emails only when triggered.

Emergency Contacts — You provide names and email addresses of your emergency contacts. This data is stored locally on your device.

Check-in Records — We record your manual and automatic check-in timestamps locally to monitor your activity status.

Email Configuration — If you configure a custom SMTP email, your email credentials are stored locally on your device for sending alert notifications.

Purchase Information — In-app purchase records are managed by Apple App Store. We only store the purchase status locally.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To monitor your safety status through check-in tracking
  • To send emergency alert emails to your designated contacts when inactivity is detected
  • To include your last known location in emergency notifications
  • To provide personalized safety settings

4. Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We do not operate any cloud servers. Your data is never uploaded to or stored on external servers controlled by us.

Email alerts are sent directly from your configured email account (or our default relay) to your emergency contacts via SMTP.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties. Your emergency contact information is only used to send alert emails when safety conditions are triggered.

6. Your Rights

  • You can delete all your data by uninstalling the App
  • You can modify or remove emergency contacts at any time
  • You can disable location services and background monitoring in your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

LoveYou is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: flashbody@foxmail.com
